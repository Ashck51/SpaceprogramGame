About the Game

Welcome to Space Project Game — a web-based 2D physics sandbox where you become the architect of the cosmos.

Place planets, stars, asteroids, and even black holes. Watch as gravity pulls, objects collide, and orbits form in real time.

Physics Engine

Realistic gravity, momentum, and collisions

Black Holes

Event horizons, accretion disks, spaghettification

Custom Objects

Drag, scale, rotate, and launch anything

Time Control

Speed up, slow down, or pause the universe

Built with HTML5, Canvas, and JavaScript — no downloads, no plugins. Just pure browser-based cosmic creation.
